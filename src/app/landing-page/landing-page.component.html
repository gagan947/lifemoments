Grow your Family Tree

Create a living family tree by adding new or existing family members, including pets and set up dedicated albums for each member. Capture life as it happens using photos, videos, notes and scanned documents and get your family's memories... finally organised.

Yearly Interviews

A standout feature is our Interview Tool, which provides tailored questions parents can use to record their child on each birthday from infancy through to age 18. These recordings are saved as a digital time capsule—creating deeply personal keepsakes to be cherished in later life.

Pass on to the Next Generation

Life’s Moments App has been designed with legacy in mind. The app introduces a unique Handover Process, allowing members to pass on a tailored copy of their Family Tree to the next generation. This ensures that family histories can continue growing over time, making Life’s Moments a truly long-term app for families.


Sharing Old Memories with Family Members

Our built-in Scanner enables users to digitise and securely store important documents such as awards, report cards and medical records directly within a person’s or pet’s profile. The scanner can also convert old printed photographs into digital format, helping families preserve memories for future generations.

Because Pets are Family and their Memories Matter

With dedicated albums ready to be filled with the thousands of photos you’ve taken, your pet’s profile(s) become meaningful keepsakes — a complete story of companionship and loyalty that you can return to for years to come.
